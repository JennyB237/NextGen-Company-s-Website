Frequently Asked Questions

What are the requirements to apply?

We welcome students from computer science, engineering, design, and related fields. Basic programming knowledge is preferred but not mandatory. Most importantly, we look for passion, curiosity, and willingness to learn.

Is the internship paid?

Yes! All our internships are paid positions. We believe in compensating our interns fairly for their valuable contributions. Stipend ranges from 10,000,per month depending on the program.

Can I do the internship remotely?

We just have hybrid options for now but we believe that with time, we will be able to offer remote too.

What happens after the internship?

High-performing interns often receive full-time job offers. Even if we don't have immediate openings, we provide strong references, portfolio reviews, and career guidance to help you land your dream job.

How long does the application process take?

The entire process typically takes 1-2 weeks from application submission to final decision. We'll keep you updated at every step and provide feedback regardless of the outcome.

Do you provide equipment and software?

Yes! We provide some necessary equipment like software licenses, and access to premium development tools and platforms. You'll have everything you need to succeed.
